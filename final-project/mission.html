A Legacy of Exploration: The History of Mars Rovers

NASA's Mars Exploration Program is a continuous journey of discovery, with each robotic rover mission building upon the technological and scientific successes of its predecessors. From the first small technology demonstration to the car-sized scientific laboratories of today, these missions have fundamentally changed our understanding of the Red Planet.

The Pathfinders: Sojourner (1997)

The Mars Pathfinder mission, which included the Sojourner rover, was a revolutionary demonstration of a low-cost, high-impact approach to planetary exploration.

The Twin Robot Geologists: Spirit and Opportunity (2004-2010/2018)

The Mars Exploration Rover (MER) mission featured two identical, golf-cart-sized rovers, Spirit and Opportunity, whose longevity and discoveries dramatically changed the scope of Mars exploration.
